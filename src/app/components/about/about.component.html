We have successfully developed an innovative e-commerce website using Angular and TypeScript, harnessing the power of modern web technologies to deliver a seamless shopping experience. Leveraging the Angular framework allowed us to build a robust and responsive front-end that ensures smooth navigation and interactions for users. One of the standout features of our project is the use of a grid system in the design, providing a visually appealing layout that showcases products beautifully while maintaining a user-friendly interface.

Our e-commerce platform offers an array of features to enhance the shopping experience. Users can easily filter and search for products, simplifying the process of finding the items they desire. Additionally, we've integrated a sophisticated price list functionality that empowers users to filter products based on their budget constraints. This feature allows customers to tailor their shopping experience to their specific needs, ensuring they find the best deals within their price range. Our Angular and TypeScript-based e-commerce website sets a new standard in online shopping, providing both functionality and aesthetics for an exceptional user experience.

Incorporating Figma, Miro, GitHub project boards, TypeScript and Angular into our project has proven to be a winning combination, facilitating a streamlined development process that results in a top-notch product.
